Mobile Banking

We are glad to introduce Mobile Banking to provide our valuable customers with the latest in service.

A registered customer can avail the following facilities through CSB Mobile banking.

  1. Balance enquiry
  2. Fund transfer between accounts within the bank.
  3. Mini statement.
  4. Utility payments-prepaid recharge, postpaid bill and other payments like water, electricity bills, DTH Top-up.
  5. Ticketing-Allows to browse, select and purchase movie and travel tickets.

All the above facilities will be available to savings bank account and current account holders on registration for the facility.

Pre-requisites for mobile handset.

  • The handset should be Java-enabled.
  • GPRS should be enabled.
  • GSM enabled handsets.
  • For CDMA enabled handsets, IVR solution is available.
