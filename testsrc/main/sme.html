SME Banking

CSB has a long history of being a SME friendly bank. CSB believes in nurturing relationships that grow with the success of the business. We offer a range of solutions tailor made for SMEs. Some of the key products that we offer

Cash Credit/ Over Draft- short-term cash loans to meet your day to day working capital needs. You can continuously draw up to the predetermined limit as and when needed.

Term Loans- long-term funds to support growth, be it capacity expansion, product diversification, plant modernization or other similar requirements. The repayment obligations are on the basis of the estimated cash flow of your business. These loans are secured against the fixed assets acquired through the loan and secondarily against suitable collateral security wherever required.

Inland Bill Discounting Facilities - offer a quick and low cost funding option. Discounting of credit worthy bills or receivables, say drawn against Letters of Credit or firm orders could be provided for a period of 3-6 months based on the tenor of the bill.

Export Finance - can be taken for procuring raw materials, manufacturing/processing, packaging and shipping goods; as Pre-shipment Credit against firm order and/or as Letter of Credit. Finance is provided either in Indian or foreign currency.

Foreign Deferred Bill Purchase/Foreign Usance Bill Purchase [FDBP/FUBP] Scheme - a Post-shipment credit facility where bill discounting can be done against export bills drawn on overseas buyers against Letters of credit or firm orders to ensure immediate liquidity.

Buyers Credit- CSB provides Buyer's credit, Import Letters of Credit, to Importers (Buyers) that can meet the payment obligations to overseas lenders i.e. Banks and Financial Institutions.

Bank Guarantees - We offer performance guarantee or financial guarantee at very competitive rates.

Letter of Credit- Apart from fund based working capital facilities, we provide a range of Non-Fund Based facilities such as Letter of credit, Bank Guarantees, Solvency certificates, etc. Generally provided for 3-6 months depending upon your trade cycle.

Import Letter of Credit for importing machinery or capital good, for tenures ranging from 1-3 years depending upon your needs.
