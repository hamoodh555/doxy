Beware of fictitious offers/lottery winnings/cheap funds offers

Customers are advised not to fall prey to fictitious offers promising huge amounts of money by unknown parties on fake letter heads of the Reserve Bank of India with fake signatures of RBI officials or by any other mode of communication.

Instances of loss of large sum of money have been reported from those who responded to the instructions of the fraudsters.

  • Do Not Pay Money to receive Large Funds from Abroad
  • Do Not fall Prey to Fictitious Offers of Funds Transfer
  • Beware of Fictitious Offers/Lottery Winnings/Cheap Fund Offers
