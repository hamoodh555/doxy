Non-Resident (External) Rupee Accounts (NRE)

CSB NRE Deposit Scheme

The Scheme that earns high interest and keeps your Rupees repatriable

JOINT A/Cs

  • Permitted with NRIs
  • A Resident close relative can also join under ‘Former or Survivor’ basis. However, the resident can operate the account against a ‘Power of Attorney’ for local disbursements only

Types

Current/Savings/Recurring/Term Deposits.

Period for Term Deposits

Minimum 12 months, Maximum 120 months

Remittances to the Account

  • Remittance to India in any permitted currency
  • Proceeds/income of investments of repatriable nature
  • Transfer from other NRE/FCNR accounts

Repatriability

Principal and interest repatriable without any restrictions, at any time

Tax

Interest income is free from Income Tax and deposit is exempted from Wealth Tax and Gift Tax

Other Important Features:

  • Deposits can be opened through remittances as DD/TT and TC/Foreign Currency or by transfer from balance in NRE accounts
  • Deposits can be transferred between banks/ branches
  • Loans available against term deposits. Repayment can be from domestic sources, if so opted
  • Nomination facility available
  • Premature withdrawals are permitted for term deposits, no penalty for premature closure (No interest, if withdrawn within the minimum stipulated period)
  • Interest is compounded quarterly for higher returns. Provision to withdraw quarterly/monthly interest
