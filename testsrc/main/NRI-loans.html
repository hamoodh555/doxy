Loan Products

  • NRI House Finance (NRI HFS) – For NRIs on regular employment or having established business with regular income. Loan for construction/acquisition of ready built house/flat for residential purpose or for repair/renovation/remodelling and/or extension of existing residential building.
  • CSB Nivas – For NRIs on regular employment or having established business with regular income. Loan for construction/acquisition of house/flat for self occupation or for repair/renovation/remodelling and/or extension of existing residential building (self occupation) or purchase of house plot and construction of house in the plot within a period of 3 years (for self occupation).
  • Car Loans – For NRIs for acquisition of non-commercial motor vehicles.
  • Casy Mithra – For NRIs for meeting personal requirements only upto Rs. 25 lakhs. The loan proceeds shall neither be repatriated nor credited to NRE/FCNR accounts.
  • CSB Property Encash – For NRIs for meeting personal requirements only upto Rs. 500 lakhs. The loan proceeds shall neither be repatriated nor credited to NRE/FCNR accounts.

For more details about NRI loans, please email your query to loans@csb.co.in, cpcschematic@csb.co.in or contact your nearest CSB Branch
