Redressal of Complaints

It has been our endeavour to give you the the best service and products. However, if you are dissatisfied with the service received, please share your grievance and we will try to resolve the issue. If you are not satisfied with our resolution, you can also approach the Banking Ombudsman or the appropriate Consumer Forum.

Details of the grievance redressal process is as below:

Step 1 - CSB Branch Manager

In case of any complaint, the matter may be first brought to the notice of the concerned Branch Manager for immediate redressal. Branch addresses are available on the Branch Network page

Step 2 - CSB Nodal Officer (Zone)

If the complaint is not redressed to the satisfaction of the customer, the matter may be taken up with the nodal officers of the zones concerned. The nodal officers contact details are available in the page for Nodal Officers in Zone.

Step 3 - CSB Principal Nodal Officer

If the complainant still feels unsatisfied with the responses received, he can address his complaint to the Bank's designated Nodal Officer at the Head Office who deals with customers complaints / grievances giving full details of the case.

Step 4 - CSB Head Office

After exhausting all the above machinery / channels, if the customer is not satisfied, he may write to Head Office and even after this, if he is not satisfied, he is free to approach the Banking Ombudsman.

Step 5 - Banking Ombudsman

The Banking Ombudsman is a quasi-judicial authority functioning under the Banking Ombudsman Scheme 2006 with the authority to enable resolution of complaints of customers of banks relating to certain services rendered by the banks. Our Bank is covered by the Banking Ombudsman Scheme, 2006 of the Reserve Bank Of India. If within 15 days of lodging a complaint with us, you do not get a satisfactory response from us, and wish to pursue other avenues for redressal of the grievance, you may approach the Banking Ombudsman appointed by the Reserve Bank Of India. For more details, please visit the website www.bankingombudsman.rbi.org.in

If the customer is not satisfied he can also approach the District Consumer Forum under Consumer Protection Act, 1985.

Anonymous complaints will not be entertained.

ATM Complaint Redressal

If you have any queries/complaints regarding ATM transactions such as non/short dispensensation of cash, card capture etc. at any Bank ATM, you may fill in the ATM complaint form (click here) and

  1. Deliver it to your branch
  2. Email us at atm@csb.co.in
  3. or call CSB Phone Banking at 1800 266 9090

If you are not a CSB customer and you have a complaint regarding non/short dispensensation of cash, card capture etc. at a CSB ATM, please take up the complaint at the respective Bank's branch.
