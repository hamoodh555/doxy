CSB KRS

CSB KRS (Kissan Raksha Scheme) is a Working Capital/ Term Loan for individuals who undertake agriculture and allied activities. The loan can be used to provide financial assistance to the farmers who require funds for meeting their farming needs including repaying of high cost loans availed from non-institutional lenders for farming purpose.

The maximum loan for this purpose can be Rs.5.00 lakh
