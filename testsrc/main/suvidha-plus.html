CSB Suvidha Plus Scheme

A corporate employees' group salary savings account, for employees drawing monthly salary of Rs.5,000/- or above

Minimum Balance: no minimum balance requirement

No. of transactions: Maximum of 10 transactions in a calendar month (deposits and withdrawals combined). For transactions in excess of the maximum limit, service charges @ Rs 5/-will be charged per transaction

ATM cum Debit card: free of charge

Multi city cheque book facility

Free Internet/Mobile Banking facility
