Inward Remittance

CSB offers several options for inward remittances/Money Transfers - a quick and safe way to transfer money from anywhere in the world to India. Enjoy instantaneous receipt of inward remittances with upto Rs. 50,000/- in cash and upto USD 2500 per transaction in your account. You can choose from the follwong Money Transfer Services - Western Union, Instant Cash, Xpress Money & Royal Money.
