Corporate Banking

The corporate banking group at CSB caters to needs of corporate entities provides a range of commercial banking products and services to corporates. This includes regular banking services corporate accounts, salary accounts, cash management services, short term funding including working capital finance, long term solutions including Term Loans, trade finance services.

Current Account

CSB enterprise Current Accounts offers the best of product features and transactional services for corporates and businesses. Enjoy the environment of a service friendly bank with a comfortable balance requirement to go along with the growth of your business enterprise.

Salary/Payroll Account

The CSB Salary/Payroll solution is a benefit-rich program with several benefits for both Employers and Employees.

The Employer enjoys with the easy disbursements of salaries (among other benefits) and the Employees benefit with features like quick credit of salaries, offers on bill payments, Phone Banking, Free Internet Banking and much more.

Working Capital Finance

CSB offers range of working capital facilities - affordable, convenient financial solutions that help in running an efficient enterprise. You can choose from the range of products including : Term Loans, Cash Credit, Letter of Credit, Bank Guarantee, Bill Discounting and more.

Trade Finance

Our export focused services include Packing Credit, Export Bills Collection / Discounting / Negotiation / Purchase / Rupee Advance, Export LC advising and Advance remittance against exports. We offer a range of services for importers too - Import Letter of Credit, Import bill Collections / Direct Bills / Advance Remittance against Imports, Buyer's Credit / Supplier's Credit. Our remittance products are very competitive - Foreign: Inward and Outward Remittances, Cheque Collection / Purchase, Demand Draft. We also offer inland products like Letter of Credit, Bank Guarantee, Bills Discounting / Purchase under LC / Non-LC, Bills for Collection.

Foreign Exchange

We provide tailored services to help coprorates make international payments, collections and investments. We also have solutions to manage the foreign exchange risks associated with international trade and investments.

Supply Chain Finance

We offer tailor made solutions that optimizes cash flow by allowing coprorates to manage their payables to their suppliers while providing the option for their suppliers to get paid through structured solutions..

Cash Management

Successful cash management involves reducing days in account receivables increasing collection rates as well as optimizing account payables and increasing days cash on hand all in order to improve a company's overall financial profitability.

Term Loans

We provide term loans for for investment in fixed assets, like plant and machinery, sheds, buildings, furniture etc., payable in installments. Repayment periods normally extend up to 8 years. However the tenure and other terms are based on specific needs of the borrower, our risk perception of the deal and are transaction specific
