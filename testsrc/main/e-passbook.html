e-Passbook

Don't worry about coming to the branch to update your passbook, now get your passbook on your smart phone.

Unlike mobile banking, you need not fill any application form- just download the application and start using it. You will need to generat an MPIN.

Features of e-Passbook:

  • All your account transactions are updated automatically
  • 3 language options available - English, Malayalam and Tamil
  • No need to wait for SMS alerts
  • Keep track of Savings, Current, Overdraft, Loans and Deposits accounts
  • Get your account statements instantly
  • You cann access the app offline too
  • Search and filter facility to find a transaction
  • You can view Multiple accounts

You may need to activate a data connection on your phone to receive this service.

With CSB, it's support all the way.
To download, click this link https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lcode.csbepassbook(Only android)
